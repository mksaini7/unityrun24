INSTRUCTIONS


  • The run is divided into two categories. 5km (Fun Run) and 11 km (Quarter Marathon).
  • Every runner must wear the given T-shirt with chest number on it.
  • The run will start from annapurna hall IIT Ropar and follow the route. here
  • There are a few refreshment booths on the route where some fruits and water will be available.
  • Every runner will get breakfast after the run.
  • Our volunteers will supervise the event at every point of the route.
  • If you break any rules at any point of time then you may be disqualified from the run. And any serious action may be taken if needed.
  • There will be volunteers on cycles with medical kit with them.
  • We recommend you to enjoy the route as much as possible by jogging and walking.
  • Collect your kit (chest number and t-shirt) from the Utility Block on 24th October (Tentative).


Event Info

  • Each runner has to report at 6:00 AM. Reporting is mandatory for certificates and awards.
  • Run will start at 6:30 AM (11 KM) and 6:40 AM (5 KM) sharp on 27th October 2024.
  • Breakfast will start at 7:30 AM in front of Annapurna Hall.
  • Award ceremony will start at 7:45 AM.
